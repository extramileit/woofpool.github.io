What charities do you support?

The charity list may evolve over time, but here is the initial list.

Best Friends

Best Friends operates the largest sanctuary for companion animals in the US located in Utah. This is a place where homeless and special-needs animals can heal, be adopted, or receive lifelong care.

They are a non-profit organization dedicated to a No-Kill mission.

Best Friends has expanded to other US cities by partnering with city shelters and rescue groups to form hubs, run adoption centers, and provide critically needed services to animals.

The American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (ASPCA)

ASPCA established the first animal human society in North America. They are a non-profit organization founded on the belief that animals are entitled to kind and respectful treatment at the hands of humans and must be protected under the law

They run adoption centers, fostering programs, spay/neuter programs, and veterinary student- and volunteer-programs

How will pool donations work?

WOOF Pool will make donations on a monthly basis provided the pool produces blocks in the preceding 30 days, i.e. every six epochs.

The charities will be be rotated after each 30 day period.

A proof of donation will be provided on this website.

As a long-term goal, WOOF pool will reach out to the organization to consider receiving ADA directly without the need to convert donations into fiat money. This will help expand the Cardano ecosystem to more organizations and promote awareness.
