Our Mission

We are a Cardano stake pool that is dedicated to the well-being of abandoned animals. Half of the profits, i.e. 50% of margin, generated by this pool will be donated to charities that can make a huge difference in the lives of animals. The donations will go mainly to shelter and rescue operations, but we welcome suggestions for charities from you. See the list of curated charities below. Please contact us with your suggested charity.

Each year, millions of companion pets are surrendered to a shelter and many are euthanized. Animals have no voice of their own, no social safety net and very few rights under the law. Shelters and rescue operations for pets need donations from the community to operate. Many animal shelters do not have the resources they need to care for sick or injured animals.

Animal shelters need food, bedding and other supplies. Many need veterinarian care, medical assistance and spaying/neutering. Your donations will help provide the care abandoned animals need until a suitable home is found for them. Together, we can make a HUGE difference.
